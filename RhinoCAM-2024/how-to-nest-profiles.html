How to Nest Profiles

Here are the basic steps to nest your profile operations.  Also refer to the Profile-NEST Quick Start Guide for more detailed instructions.

1.Load the part file containing your geometry to nest.

2.Draw a rectangle on the XY plane representing your sheet stock size.  This is not required but it make the process easier to understand.

3.Move the part geometry that you want to nest outside of the stock perimeter.

4.Define the Machine Setup.

5.Select a Post-Processor.

6.Define your Sheet to Nest.

7.Define your Box Stock size to match your Sheet Size.

8.Align your Part and Stock if needed.

9.Select a Stock Material.

10.Create a 2 1/2 Axis Profile operation for each of your parts.

11.Set your Nesting Parameters and Execute the Nest.

12.Review/Edit/Simulate your nested sheets.

13.Generate a Nest Report if needed.

14.Post G-Code for each Nested Sheet.

How to check/update your Graphics Display Driver (i.e., your Graphics Card)

I want you to make sure your drivers for your graphics adapter in your PC are up to date.  Here are the steps to do this:

1.Close all applications.

2.Select Start > Control Panel

3.Select Hardware & Sound

4.Under Devices & Printers, select Device Manager

5.Expand the Display Adapters section and you will see your graphics adapter for example "NVIDIA GeForce 9300 GE".  Yours will likely be a different graphics adapter.

6.Right-click on the graphics adapter and select "Update Drive Software ..."

7.After the drivers are updated, reboot your PC.

8.Run VisualCAM again and test.

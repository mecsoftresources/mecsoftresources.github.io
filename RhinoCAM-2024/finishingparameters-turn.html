Dialog Box: Global Parameters tab, Turn Finishing
Cut Control

Final finishing cut can be specified in terms of the total passes of the cutter over the stock.

Cut Control - Allows specifying multiple passes for finishing. Selecting Multiple Passes allows user to set the Total Cut Depth and Depth/Cut.

The direction of cut can be either unidirectional or Bi-directional (Zig-Zag)

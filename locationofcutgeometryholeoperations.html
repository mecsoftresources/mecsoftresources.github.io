Location of Cut Geometry, Hole Operations

Select from the following for Location of Drill Point(s):

At Top  

This uses the Z location of the selected Control Geometry as the top of cut. The generated cuts will start at this Z location and cut down in Z to the specified total Drill Depth.  At Top is typically used when your hole diameters or drill points are at the top of your part.  

At Bottom  

This uses the Z location of the selected Control Geometry as the bottom of cut. The generated cuts will be above the selected machining region and last cut would be at the Z location of the specified region. At Bottom is typically used when you select the diameter or drill points are at the bottom of the hole.

Project to 3D Model  

This option projects the selected the Control Geometry to the underlying 3D geometry.  

Pick

This allows you to specify the Top of Cut for the selected Control Geometry and is typically used when the selected region is not at top or bottom of the hole. The generated cuts will start at this specified Z location and cut down in Z to the specified Drill Depth. This would be useful when the selected Control Geometry is at the bottom edge of a fillet or chamfer.

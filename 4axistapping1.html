Tapping, 4 Axis

This method is used to make threaded holes. The 4 axis Tap operation is invoked by selecting the Program tab, clicking on the Holes button in the Machining Browser and selecting the 4 Axis Tap operation.

Tap Types

The tap cycles supported are:

Standard Tap
This is a standard tap cycle.  Move to XY, Move to Z at approach distance, tap to depth, retract to clearance.

Peck Tapping  
This is a peck tap cycle.  Move to XY, Move to Z at approach distance, tap to peck depth, retract to top of peck, tap to peck depth, repeat to until full tap depth, retract clearance.

User Defined Tap1, 2, 3, 4  
This is a tap cycle that you can define using the User Defined Cycles in the Post-Processor Generator.

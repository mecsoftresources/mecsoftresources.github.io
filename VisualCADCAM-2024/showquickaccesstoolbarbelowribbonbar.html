Show Quick Access Toolbar below Ribbon Bar

You can move the Quick Access Toolbar to reside below the Ribbon bar by selecting Show Below Ribbon from the Customize Quick Access Toolbar menu.  You can also do this by selecting appropriate check box in the Customize dialog.

Create a Revolved Surface

Create a surface by revolving a curve (or surface edge) around an axis.  Start and end angles may be entered according to the right-hand rule of positive rotation, as determined by the direction that the axis line is defined.

 

Command Prompts
Basic Procedure

 

Screen Pick

Command Input

Step 1

The curves (or surface edges) may be pre-selected.

 

Step 2

(Optional) Enter X, Y, [Z] coordinates for the first point (start of axis line).

Step 3

(Optional) Enter X, Y, [Z] coordinates for the second point (end of axis line).

Step 4

 

Enter the start angle of revolution (according to the right-hand rule of positive rotation).

Step 5

 

Enter the end angle of revolution.

 

Final


Notes

1.The direction of definition of the axis line is important because it determines the right-hand rule of positive rotation for the start and end angles of the revolved surface.

2.The curve's (or surfaces edge's) existing position defines the zero degree position for angles.

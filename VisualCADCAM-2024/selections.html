Selections Pane, Modeling Aids Ribbon Bar

The Modeling Aids Ribbon Bar contains the following Selections pane with associated functions.

Modeling Aids Ribbon Bar
Selections Pane

These commands allow you to select certain geometry to apply commands to as a group.  Commands such as Delete or Scale will apply to all of the currently selected geometry in the same way and at the same time.  Selected geometry will be highlighted with the highlight color.  The default highlight color is yellow.  Selected items can also be on multiple layers.

Multiple items can be selected by holding down the control key (on the keyboard) and left-clicking on the item with the mouse.

 

Selections do not apply to geometries that area hidden, locked and on layers that are not visible and or locked.

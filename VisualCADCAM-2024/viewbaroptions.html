View Toolbar

The View Toolbar contains easily accessible commands that control the appearance of the currently active view.  This includes motion of the camera as well as how objects are displayed. When a view change is invoked from the View Toolbar toolbar, the existing view magnification factor is maintained. The View Toolbar is displayed in the currently active Viewport.

Once a machining operation is created, you can step through the toolpath motions using the Toolpath Viewer. To display the  viewer, double click on the toolpath icon of the operation in the Machining Browser. The toolpath viewer is a dockable dialog bar that will be initially docked next to the Machining Browser.
